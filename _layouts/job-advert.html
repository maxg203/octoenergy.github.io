About Octopus Energy

Octopus Energy is a new energy supplier for the UK. Through smart use of the latest technology, we’re changing the way people consume and buy energy, and putting customers first.

We’re backed by Octopus Investments, who have an investment portfolio worth over £7bn and over £1.5bn invested in renewable energy generation assets. In total, Octopus owns about 40% of the UK’s commercial solar generation capacity.

Octopus Energy is an ambitious young business already making real change in the market, embracing the benefits of being a startup with major financial backing.
